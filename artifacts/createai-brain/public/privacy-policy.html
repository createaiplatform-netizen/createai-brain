Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026 · Lakeside Trinity LLC

Lakeside Trinity LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates CreateAI Brain (the "Platform"), accessible via web browser, mobile PWA installation, and any associated native applications. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our Platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account registration data (name, email address, company name)
  • Business data you enter into Platform modules (CRM records, financial data, project information)
  • Communications with us via email or support channels
  • Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store full card numbers)

We also collect information automatically, including:

  • Device type, operating system, and browser version
  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Usage patterns within the Platform (features accessed, frequency of use)
  • Error logs and performance data to improve the Platform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Platform and its features
  • To process transactions and send related information (receipts, confirmations)
  • To send service-related notifications and Platform updates
  • To respond to comments, questions, and support requests
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or illegal activity
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your business data to train AI models.

3. AI Processing

CreateAI Brain uses OpenAI's GPT-4o API to power the AI Strategy Engine and other generative features. When you use these features, your input is transmitted to OpenAI for processing. This is governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not share identifiable personal information with OpenAI beyond what you explicitly enter into AI-powered prompts.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Replit's infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) for all data
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive credentials
  • Access controls limiting who can access production data
  • Regular security assessments

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at admin@lakesidetrinity.com.

6. Sharing of Information

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: OpenAI (AI processing), Stripe (payments), Resend (email), Twilio (SMS), Replit (infrastructure)
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Data portability (export your data in machine-readable format)
  • Objection to or restriction of processing
  • Withdrawal of consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at admin@lakesidetrinity.com.

8. Children's Privacy

CreateAI Brain is intended for business use by adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers in accordance with applicable law.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
