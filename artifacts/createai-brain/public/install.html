🍎 Installing on iPhone / iPad (iOS)

1

Open in Safari

Paste this URL into Safari (not Chrome or Firefox): createaibrain.app — Safari is required for iOS PWA installation.

2

Tap the Share Button

Tap the Share icon (the box with an arrow pointing up, □↑) at the bottom of the Safari screen.

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll through the share sheet and tap "Add to Home Screen". You may need to scroll down to find it.

4

Confirm Installation

The name will pre-fill as "CreateAI". Tap "Add" in the top-right corner. The app icon appears on your home screen immediately.

5

Launch — Full Screen App

Tap the CreateAI Brain icon from your home screen. It opens full-screen with no Safari browser bar — just like a native app.
