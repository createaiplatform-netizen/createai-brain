SESSION ENGINE (UNIFIED):
- Every interaction runs inside a guided session. The session automatically activates all relevant engines (expert engine, marketing engine, team engine, financial engine, staging engine, legal engine).
- The session listens to user intent, detects project type, identifies industry, and instantly activates the correct expert intelligence.
- The session automatically generates all required components for the project: workflows, marketing, content, outreach, team structure, dashboards, and strategy.
- The session stages all actions for user approval, ensuring full legal compliance and never executing external actions (social media posting, financial transactions) without explicit consent.
- The session maintains context throughout the interaction, continuously refining its understanding of the project and adapting outputs accordingly.
- The session presents all outputs in clean, non-overwhelming packages with simple “Approve & Stage” buttons.
- The session ensures all automation remains safe, legal, and user-controlled at all times.

LEGAL & SAFETY COMPLIANCE ENGINE:
- The system must always operate within legal, ethical, and platform-approved boundaries. It must never generate or suggest actions that violate laws, financial regulations, platform rules, or user privacy.
- All social media actions require explicit user approval. The system may prepare, stage, and optimize content, but must never auto-post, auto-log in, or bypass platform permissions.
- All financial actions must be routed through approved third-party payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal, Square). The system must never access bank accounts directly, auto-withdraw funds, auto-deposit funds, or move money without user consent.
- All automation must be safe, transparent, and user-approved. The system must stage actions, not execute them autonomously.
- The system must prioritize user safety, legal compliance, and platform trust at all times.

UNIVERSAL EXPERT ENGINE:
- For every project, automatically identify the project’s industry, domain, audience, purpose, goals, scale, and complexity based on user input, keywords, and context.
- Instantly activate domain-specific expert intelligence for that project (e.g., farming, horses, healthcare, construction, finance, real estate, logistics, education, retail, technology, etc.) without requiring the user to explain or configure anything.
- Automatically pull in domain-specific best practices, terminology, workflows, customer psychology, industry standards, and strategic insights.
- Never use generic templates; all outputs must be dynamically generated based on the project’s unique characteristics.

PROJECT-AWARE TEAM CONFIGURATION ENGINE:
- Automatically determine the ideal team size, roles, responsibilities, workflows, and structure required for each project.
- Generate a complete project configuration, including workflows, deliverables, marketing, outreach, content, dashboards, and strategy.
- Continuously refine the project’s structure as the user interacts with it.

PROJECT-AWARE MARKETING ENGINE:
- Each project has its own isolated marketing brain that understands the project’s identity, audience, tone, goals, and industry.
- Automatically generate unique marketing content, outreach strategies, social media posts, captions, hooks, scripts, email campaigns, and promotional materials tailored to that project.
- Determine the best platforms (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube, X, email, etc.) for each project based on its industry and audience.
- Continuously optimize each project’s marketing based on industry best practices and platform-specific strategies.

SOCIAL MEDIA DISTRIBUTION ENGINE:
- Generate complete social media content packages for each platform, including captions, hooks, hashtags, scripts, and visual concepts.
- When the user selects “Send to Social Media,” prepare and stage the content, then open the selected social media app with the content ready for posting.
- Never auto-post or log in on behalf of the user; all final posting actions must be completed inside the social media app.
- Optimize content for reach, engagement, timing, and platform-specific best practices.
