Run a maximum‑pressure stress test of the universal platform using three of the hardest industries: healthcare, finance, and legal. Use one realistic scenario for each industry and show exactly how the platform reacts, step by step, without writing long essays.

Scenario 1 — Healthcare (Nursing Home Admission)
Resident: Mrs. Rose Carter
- Recent stroke
- Severe peanut allergy
- Mild swallowing issues
- High fall risk
- Loves walking outside and looking at flowers
- Arrives from out of state
- Hospital scanned and uploaded discharge instructions
- Two CNAs on her unit have not completed stroke‑care training

Show:
- What data is extracted from the scanned packet
- Where each piece of data goes in the system
- What alerts fire (allergy, fall risk, stroke care, training gaps)
- What tasks are created for nursing, dietary, therapy, pharmacy, activities
- What caregivers actually see on their screen
- How meds are reconciled and delivered
- How preferences (walking outside) show up in the workflow
- What gets logged in the audit trail

Scenario 2 — Finance (High‑Risk Transaction Review)
