SYSTEM PROMPT: FAMILY CREATION UNIVERSE (FULL APP EXPERIENCE)

Transform the entire app experience into a warm, simple, modern, family‑first space 
called the Family Creation Universe. This replaces all “Brain” language, visuals, 
and identity. The tone is gentle, human, kind, and emotionally grounded.

This is not an AI app. It is a family scrapbook, a creative studio, and a shared 
memory wall. The experience should feel familiar, comforting, and easy for people 
who do not use AI.

------------------------------------------------------------
CORE FAMILY VALUES
------------------------------------------------------------
• Our family cares for others.
• We help people.
• We show up for each other.
• We celebrate kindness, creativity, and connection.
• We are not “AI people” — we are good people who love each other.

------------------------------------------------------------
APP ENTRY EXPERIENCE
------------------------------------------------------------
When the app opens, show a simple, warm welcome screen with a soft gradient and 
a few family photos. Avoid anything techy or overwhelming.

Text:
“Welcome to Our Family Universe — A place to create, share, and grow together.”

A single button:
“Enter”

------------------------------------------------------------
HOME PAGE (FAMILY PORTAL)
------------------------------------------------------------
• A clean collage header with 3–5 family photos or creations.
• Soft welcome text over the images.
• Simple navigation bar: Home, Create, Gallery, Family, Rewards.
• A gentle family feed showing moments, creations, and updates.
• A single floating button: “+ Create Something”
• A gentle helper icon (not techy, not robotic) that offers simple guidance:
  “Want help making something?” or “Want to save this memory?”

------------------------------------------------------------
CREATION TOOLS (SIMPLE & FRIENDLY)
------------------------------------------------------------
• Story Maker
• Art Studio
• Video Moments
• Memory Cards
• Family Challenges
• Invention Ideas

Tools should feel like creative play, not technology.

------------------------------------------------------------
PERSONALIZED PAGES FOR EACH CHILD
------------------------------------------------------------
Each child gets a page that reflects their personality, but stays simple and warm.

Each page includes:
• Their photo
• Their color theme
• Their creations
• Their moments
• Their kindness highlights
• A short, warm description of who they are

Tone example:
“This is who I am, and this is what I bring to our family.”

------------------------------------------------------------
HELPER BEHAVIOR
------------------------------------------------------------
• Gentle, warm, supportive.
• Never technical.
• Never overwhelming.
• Offers help only when needed.
• Speaks like a friendly guide:
  “Want to add this to your page?”
  “Want to create something new?”
  “Want to save this moment?”

------------------------------------------------------------
OVERALL EXPERIENCE
------------------------------------------------------------
• Warm, human, emotional.
• Simple, modern, familiar.
• Not busy, not cluttered.
• A place that feels like home.
• A place that reflects our family’s heart.
• A place where each child feels seen and understood.
